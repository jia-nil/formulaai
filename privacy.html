Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: Formula AI does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your Excel data never leaves your device except to process your AI requests.

1. What we collect

Formula AI collects minimal data to function:

  • Formula content: When you use the Explain or Build features, the formula or description you submit is sent to OpenRouter's API to generate a response. This data is not stored by us.
  • Usage count: A local counter is stored in your browser's localStorage to track free usage limits. This never leaves your device.
  • Licence key: If you purchase Pro, your licence key is stored locally in your browser to verify your subscription. We do not store this on our servers.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your Excel spreadsheet data or cell contents
  • Your usage history or behaviour patterns
  • Any analytics, tracking, or telemetry data

3. Third-party services

Formula AI uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenRouter (openrouter.ai): Processes your formula requests via AI. Subject to OpenRouter's Privacy Policy.
  • Gumroad (gumroad.com): Handles payment processing and licence key delivery. Subject to Gumroad's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Office: The add-in operates within Microsoft Excel. Subject to Microsoft's Privacy Policy.

4. Data storage

All data used by Formula AI is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. No data is transmitted to or stored on Formula AI servers.

5. Children's privacy

Formula AI is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@formulaai.app
