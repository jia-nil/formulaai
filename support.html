🚀 Getting started

1
Install the add-in from Microsoft AppSource — search "Formula AI" or use the direct link.
2
Open the add-in in Excel via Insert → Add-ins → My Add-ins → Formula AI.
3
Explain a formula: Click any cell with a formula, then click "Explain this formula" in the sidebar.
4
Build a formula: Switch to the Build tab, describe what you need in plain English, and click "Build my formula."
